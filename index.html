The Original Off-Grid Festival

Non-profit Low Impact Festival

Join us for four days of low impact living in an area of outstanding beauty, every nook and corner bursting with music, creativity, natural abundance and community spirit.

The original off-grid,non-profit festival: we’re powered by sun, wind, people and passion for change.Intimate solar stages showcase up and coming acts, legendary festival favourites, DJs and spellbinding raconteurs.

Share wisdom and skills; find friends old and new; weave, weld and tell stories.
